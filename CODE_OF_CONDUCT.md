# Code of Conduct

## The short version

Be decent to people. This is a small hobby project about counting beats per minute —
nothing here is worth being unpleasant over.

## What's expected

- Be welcoming. Plenty of people building this are learning electronics or C++ for the
  first time, and "how do I flash it" is a perfectly good question.
- Give feedback on the work, not the person.
- Assume good faith. Most friction online is a misread tone, not malice.
- Accept that maintainers may decline a change. A "no" to a feature isn't a judgement
  of you.

## What isn't acceptable

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Discriminatory language or imagery of any kind
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Persistent disruption of discussions or issues

## Scope

This applies in the issue tracker, pull requests, discussions, and anywhere someone is
representing the project.

## Reporting

If someone's behaviour is a problem, contact the maintainer at
**kyle@ohmic-labs.co.uk**. Reports are handled discreetly.

Maintainers may edit, lock, or delete contributions that breach this document, and may
block persistent offenders.
